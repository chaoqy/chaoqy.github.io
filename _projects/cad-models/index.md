# Linear Induction Motor
Modeled a double-sided 3-phase 9-coil linear induction motor along with a mounting system and winding rig. Conducted static analyses on the mounting system to ensure that all structural components meet the required safety factors and load-bearing capacities under expected operational conditions. Optimized the mounting system design by reducing material usage after initial analysis showed a factor of safety exceeding 20, improving resource efficiency while maintaining structural integrity.
